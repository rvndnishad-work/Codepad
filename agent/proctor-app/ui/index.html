Screen integrity check

Before your interview begins, this app checks that no hidden AI-assist or answer-overlay tools are running on your screen. Please review and consent.

What this checks

  • 🔎Open windows that hide themselves from screen sharing (the trick AI-cheat overlays use).
  • 🪟Always-on-top transparent overlays sitting over your screen.
  • 🏷️Known assist-tool app names from a published list.

What it never does

  • 🚫It does not capture screenshots, keystrokes, files, or your personal content.
  • 🚫It sends only a risk score and flagged window names — nothing else.
  • 🧹It removes itself automatically when the interview ends.

By starting, you agree to this screen check for the duration of your interview.
